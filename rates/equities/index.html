US Equities — Indices, Volatility & Valuation

The four major US equity benchmarks (S&P 500, Nasdaq Composite, Dow, Russell 2000), the VIX as the market’s fear gauge, the S&P 500’s drawdown from its all-time high, and a market-wide valuation gauge: the Wilshire 5000 divided by NIPA after-tax corporate profits, a quarterly “P/E” using economy-wide earnings as the denominator. Daily indices via Yahoo Finance; Nasdaq, VIX, and quarterly profits from BEA via FRED.
