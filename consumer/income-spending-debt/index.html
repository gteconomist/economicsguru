Income, Spending, & Debt

How US households are earning, spending, saving, and borrowing — personal income & consumption (nominal and real), saving rate, personal interest payments, total consumer credit (less mortgage), revolving debt, and 90+ day delinquency rates. Monthly data from BEA via FRED; quarterly debt and delinquency data from the NY Fed's Quarterly Report on Household Debt and Credit.

Personal Income & Consumption — Nominal MoM

Personal Income & Consumption — Real MoM
(% change from preceding month; chained 2017 dollars — deflated by the PCE price index)

Personal Saving Rate
