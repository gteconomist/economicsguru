Overview
Three ways the U.S. measures inflation — consumer prices (CPI), producer prices (PPI), and the PCE deflator.
CPI
Headline & core CPI, monthly change, components, energy prices. Source: BLS.
PPI
Final-demand prices producers receive — headline, core, goods, services. Source: BLS.
PCE
The Fed's preferred gauge — headline, core, services, supercore. Source: BEA.
