Manufacturing & Production Surveys (ISM, Cass)

Industry Surveys — ISM, Cass & NFIB

The forward-looking layer on top of the hard manufacturing data: monthly diffusion indices from the Institute for Supply Management (Manufacturing and Services PMIs and their sub-indices), the Cass Freight Index from Cass Information Systems — a volume gauge built from billions of dollars of payments processed each month — and the NFIB Small Business Optimism and Uncertainty Indices, which capture how main-street America is feeling. PMIs above 50 = expansion; below 50 = contraction. NFIB Optimism above 98 (the 52-year average) = small business confidence above trend.

ISM Manufacturing Index
(Diffusion Index, Seasonally Adjusted; 50 = Expansion / Contraction Threshold; Long-Run History from 1948)
Source: Institute for Supply Management (ISM) — Manufacturing PMI. Long-run average ~52; recessions push the index into the low-40s or below. Download CSV
ISM Manufacturing Index — Major Components
(Diffusion Indices; Bars Represent Distance From 50; SA — Total / Employment / New Orders / Backlog / Commodity Prices)
Source: Institute for Supply Management (ISM). Backlog of Orders begins 1993, Commodity Prices begins 2003 (Macrobond series start). Download CSV
ISM Services Index
(Diffusion Index, Seasonally Adjusted — Composite + Employment / New Orders / Prices Sub-Indices)
Source: Institute for Supply Management (ISM) — Services Report on Business. Composite begins July 1997. Download CSV
Composite PMI — Manufacturing vs. Services
(ISM Manufacturing Total + ISM Services Composite on One Chart, with the 50 Threshold; Quick Read on Whether the Whole Economy Is Expanding)
Source: Institute for Supply Management (ISM) — Manufacturing and Services PMIs. Services dominates US output (~80% of GDP) but Manufacturing tends to lead at cycle turning points. Download CSV
Cass Freight Shipments — Year-Over-Year %
(Volume Index, % Change vs. Same Month a Year Ago; Cycle View — Negative for Most of the Post-2022 Freight Recession)
Source: Computed in-house from Cass Information Systems Volume Index. Negative bars (coral) = freight contracting vs. last year; positive (navy) = freight expanding. Download CSV
NFIB Small Business Optimism Index
(Diffusion-Style Index, Seasonally Adjusted; 1986 = 100; Long-Run History from Late 1974, with the 52-Year ~98 Average Shown as a Dashed Reference Line)
Source: National Federation of Independent Business (NFIB) Research Foundation — Small Business Economic Trends (SBET). Released the second Tuesday of each month based on a survey of NFIB member firms. Download CSV
NFIB Small Business Uncertainty Index
(Net %, Seasonally Adjusted; Higher = More Uncertain; Historical Average ~68 Shown as a Dashed Reference Line; Series Begins 1986)
Source: National Federation of Independent Business (NFIB) Research Foundation — Small Business Economic Trends. Built from owners' net responses to six forward-looking SBET questions. Download CSV
NFIB — Single Most Important Problem (Latest Survey)
(Share of Small Business Owners Citing Each Issue as Their Top Problem; NSA; Snapshot Updates Monthly with Each SBET Release)
Source: National Federation of Independent Business (NFIB) Research Foundation — SBET. The nine published categories rarely sum to 100% — the residual is shown as “Other / none”. Download CSV
