“Golden Handcuff”: 30-Year Mortgage Rate vs Effective Rate on Outstanding Mortgage Debt
(Monthly; 30Y = Freddie Mac PMMS monthly avg; Effective rate on outstanding mortgage debt, owner- and tenant-occupied residential housing)
Sources: Freddie Mac via FRED — MORTGAGE30US (weekly, collapsed to monthly mean). U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis (NIPA: Mortgage interest paid, owner- and tenant-occupied residential housing) via Haver Analytics — Moody’s-calculated effective rate, mnemonic IR%BEMERAQ.IUSA. Mortgage Debt Outstanding
(Total Mortgage Balance Component of Household Debt; Quarterly, $ Trillions, NSA)
Source: Federal Reserve Bank of New York — Quarterly Report on Household Debt and Credit (HHDC), mortgage component, via Haver mnemonic MBLNYHDDBCMUQ.IUSA. NY Fed HHDC publishes mid-quarter with full Q1 2026 coverage where FRED’s Z.1 HHMSDODNS only has a 13-month trailing window. Download CSV
Housing Affordability Index
(NAR Fixed-Rate Housing Affordability Index, seasonally adjusted; 100 = median family income exactly qualifies for the median-priced home)
Source: National Association of Realtors. History from Haver mnemonics HXAFFFM.IUSA (Moody's SA) + XHXAFFFM.IUSA (NSA), Jan 1981 onward. New months scraped from the NAR press release via Tavily; in-house monthly seasonal factors derived from the 36-month CSV overlap are applied to scraped NSA to yield SA estimates that match Moody's to ~0.01 pt. Download CSV
Median Home Price ÷ Median Household Income
(Quarterly ratio; NAR median existing-home price ÷ quarterly median HH income; both nominal, current dollars)
Sources: National Association of Realtors — median existing-home sales price (monthly NSA, collapsed to quarterly mean), shared with the existing-homes page. Moody's Analytics quarterly median HH income (Haver mnemonic RYHHMEDQ.IUSA, derived from Census BOC ACS / CPS Table FINC-01) seeded as the canonical historical record 1970Q1 onward; for years past Moody's coverage the going-forward path uses Census P-60 annual nominal HH income (FRED MEHOINUSA646N) multiplied by intra-year shape factors derived from Moody's 2010+ quarterly pattern (Q1=0.986, Q2=0.996, Q3=1.005, Q4=1.012).
